About Me

I’m Patrick, a software engineer and lifelong builder with roots in South Carolina. My background started in my family’s cabinet shop, where I learned craftsmanship, precision, and how to break down complex problems into workable steps. From there I explored a wide range of hands‑on fields—cabinet design, engineering, installation, industrial maintenance, and carpentry—before finding the path that finally felt like mine: software and game development.

Today I focus on creating small, thoughtful projects that help me grow as an engineer and as a designer. I’m driven by the idea of building experiences that reach people at scale, especially in games. I want to contribute to projects that entertain, inspire, and leave an impact.

I’m honest, curious, and motivated by the feeling that I’m catching up to the life I want. Every project on this site is a step toward that goal.
